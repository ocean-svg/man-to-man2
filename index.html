Why This Matters

Informal traders are being left behind by digital commerce

South Africa's township economy is worth nearly a trillion rand — yet the traders driving it are excluded from the platforms, payment systems, and financial tools that everyone else takes for granted.

⚠️

No secure payment infrastructure

Most sellers rely on cash, unprotected EFT, or WhatsApp coordination — leaving them exposed to fraud and making buyers nervous about completing transactions.

🤔

No verified identity or trust signals

Without verified profiles, buyers have no way to distinguish legitimate sellers from scammers. This kills conversion rates and caps what sellers can charge.

🏦

Locked out of financial systems

No transaction history means no credit score, no business account, no loan access. Informal traders are permanently stuck in "start from zero" mode.

🌍

Platforms built for someone else

Facebook Marketplace, Gumtree, and OLX weren't designed for township realities — no language support, no local logistics, no low-data mode.

The Opportunity

A R900 billion economy without a home

The informal sector isn't small — it employs 1 in 5 South Africans. It just needs infrastructure that meets it where it is, not where Silicon Valley imagines it to be.

Township economy size R 900B / year
Informal sector employment share ~20%
E-commerce participation gap Massive

Security & Trust

We take fraud seriously
so you don't have to

Trust isn't built through good intentions — it's built through systems. Every layer of MzansiMarket is designed to make fraud structurally difficult.

🏦

Escrow Payment System

Buyer funds are held by a regulated financial intermediary until both parties confirm the transaction. Neither side can run off with the money.

🪪

FICA / KYC Identity Checks

Every seller undergoes South African ID verification before they can list. Verified sellers show a badge — anonymous listings aren't possible.

⚖️

Built-In Dispute Resolution

If something goes wrong, our resolution process is clear, fast, and fair. Funds are never released until disputes are settled.

🔐

Penetration-Tested Platform

Full security testing before every major release. We treat your data and your money with the same seriousness as a bank.

⭐

Verified Ratings System

Only confirmed buyers can leave reviews. Ratings are transaction-verified and can't be gamed or bought.

📜

Transaction Records

Every deal is logged and downloadable. Sellers build a verifiable income history — groundwork for formal credit access.
